My Love for Cinematography

There's something magical about the way a story unfolds through the camera's lens. Cinematography isn't just about pointing a camera and hitting record—it's about crafting emotion, perspective, and meaning through lighting, angles, and motion.

I've always been fascinated by how filmmakers use visual language to express emotion. Films like Everything Everywhere All At Once, Joker, and a new favorite of mine, Sinners have shown me the power of deliberate visual choices. Every frame is a painting, every scene a canvas of feelings.

In my free time, I'm an avid movie goer and love seeing director's visions come to life. Cinematography has shaped how I see the world—both behind and beyond the lens.
