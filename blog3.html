Concert Recap: Riot Fest 2023

Riot Fest is located in Chicago. It was my first time traveling far with a friend but Chicago was beautiful.

The first day was nerve-wracking, it was the first time I've ever been to a music festival and was overwhelmed with all the stages, bands, food, and people. However as the day progressed, my nerves melted away. I even got a few compliments while walking to each set.

As people gathered early to prepare for the last act of the night, Foo Fighter artist finally stepped out, the entire place erupted. Phones were out, people were crying, screaming, singing; it was magic. The stage production was incredible: lights synced with the beat, smoke effects, and some insane visual backdrops.

I saw so many great bands for the next 3 days from PUP, to Sleep Token, Insane Clown Posse, and The Cure! I had the most fun seeing Sleep Token and ICP back to back. From soulful, entranced singing of Sleep Token to getting sprayed with diet Faygo and unexpectedly getting hit with a full 2 litre that I ended keeping as a souvenir from ICP.

By the end, my feet were tired and sore but my heart was full. If you ever get a chance to go to a musical festival, GO. You won't regret it.
